4.5. Shortcuts
==============

+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| operations           | shortcuts                        |
+======================+==================================+
| Next checkbox        | ``Tab`` or ``down``              |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| Previous checkbox    | ``up``                           |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| Check/uncheck        | ``space``                        |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| input file predict   | ``Shift``\ +\ ``Enter``          |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| new line             | ``\``                            |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| clear all            | ``Ctrl``\ +\ ``x``               |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| manual input predict | ``Enter``                        |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| save data            | ``Ctrl``\ +\ ``Shift``\ +\ ``s`` |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
| save image           | ``Ctrl``\ +\ ``s``               |
+----------------------+----------------------------------+
